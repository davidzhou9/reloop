About RELOOP

RELOOP is a project designed to improve campus sustainability. It allows facilities workers to provide feedback on the waste disposal of residential college rooms and correct improper disposal habits.

What kinds of paper can I recycle?

Recycle office paper and mail, cardboard and paperboard, food and beverage cartons, newspaper, magazines, posters, paper bags, and both paperback and hardcover books.

What kinds of glass and metal can I recycle?

Recycle empty plastic bottles, jugs, and tubs (foil lids like for yogurt should be removed), empty glass bottles and jars, and aluminum, steel/tin cans, lids, and serving trays.

What should not be recycled?

DO NOT recycle paper kitchen products, food waste, coffee cups, plastic clamshell containers, plastic utensils, plastic wrappers, coated papers, styrofoam, broken glass, or ceramics.
